🚀 Next 5 Things to Build (Highest Impact for Cyber Cafe)
1

✅ Checkbox / Declaration auto-tick

Most government forms end with "I declare that above information is correct" checkbox. Auto-tick Terms & Conditions and declaration checkboxes. Impact: Every form needs this.

2

🖨️ One-click Print button in extension

After filling form, show a "🖨️ Print this page" button in popup. Saves the operator from manually Ctrl+P. Impact: Every submission ends with a printout.

3

📋 Applied forms log per customer

Record which portals/forms each customer applied to, with date + cost. Show in customer profile. Impact: Operators can track work done, customer can re-print anytime.

4

🏛️ E-Shram + PM Kisan profile fields

Add farmer-specific fields (Khasra no, Khatauni no, land area, bank passbook) for E-Shram/PM Kisan/Ayushman forms. Impact: 50%+ of cyber cafe work is these schemes.

5

💳 Auto UPI payment (Razorpay)

Cafe owner pays directly via UPI QR — wallet credited instantly without admin approval wait. Impact: Removes friction, faster recharge = more usage.
