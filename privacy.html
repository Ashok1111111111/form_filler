3. How the Chrome Extension Works

The AI Form Filler Chrome Extension:

  • Reads form fields only on the currently active tab when you click "Auto-Fill"
  • Sends field labels (not values) to OpenAI's API via our proxy server to identify which customer data goes where
  • Fills fields locally in your browser — no form data is stored on our servers
  • Detects document upload fields to show you resize requirements
  • Does not run in the background or track your browsing

📋 Form field data (what gets filled into forms) is processed locally in your browser. Only the field labels/names are sent to AI for mapping — never the actual personal values.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored on Google Firebase — encrypted at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth — we never see or store your password
  • Customer data is tied to your account and only accessible by you
  • Firebase security rules ensure no other user can access your data

6. Data Retention

  • Customer data: Kept until you delete it or close your account
  • Transaction records: Kept for 3 years for billing purposes
  • Auth tokens: Cleared when you log out
